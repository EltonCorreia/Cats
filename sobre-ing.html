The Cats was founded in 2001 in the neighborhood of Petrolândia in Minas Gerais in the back of the garage of the home of the parents of the visinarian and current CEO, Anna Luisa. When she founded the company that today is considered one of the largest in its field, she was just 21 years old, with a cat called Carlota, a bag of Cheetos, an old computer and a dream: to start her own business.


Currently the Cats is one of Petrolândia's largest operating companies, offering cutting-edge technology that is accessible to society. Cats offers computers and cell phones to the city's public school students to advance their studies and open up better opportunities in the job market.
